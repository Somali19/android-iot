ABOUT

GIET University



GIET University, Gunupur, the flagship of the famous Gandhi Group of Institutions of Odisha. It is 23-years old Autonomous college accredited by NBA 5 (five times) and also by NAAC (twice) with A+ grade with latest CGPA being 3.28/4.0. Due to its excellent track record, the Government of Odisha has accorded with private state University in December 2018.

It is one of the oldest AICTE approved autonomous engineering college in Eastern India, situated on the southern edge of Odisha, adjacent to north-coastal Andhra Pradesh. GIET University, Gunupur the symbol of quality education in Eastern India is sensitive to the growing trends of globalization in higher education.

Android and IOT Club



In order to expand the reach of Android and Internet of Things (IOT) across the campus, department of Computer Science and Engineering established the Android and IOT Club. The purpose of this club is to help the student to learn and educate all so that individuals will learn about Android Development and Internet Of Things. Trainings , workshops , webinars and community service activities are provided here.

It is a place to gather all like minded people to develop their career in the field of Android and IOT.
